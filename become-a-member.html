Become a NYAN Member Today

The Nigerian Youth Assembly for National Development (NYAN) welcomes passionate young Nigerians who are committed to making a positive impact in their communities and contributing to national development. By becoming a member, you'll join a network of like-minded individuals working together to create a better future for Nigeria.

Membership Benefits

  • Access to exclusive leadership development programs
  • Networking opportunities with influential leaders
  • Participation in national and international conferences
  • Opportunities to lead community development projects
  • Mentorship from experienced professionals
  • Access to resources and support for personal growth
  • Representation in national youth policy discussions
  • Certificate of membership and official ID card
  • Regular updates on youth development initiatives
  • Opportunity to run for leadership positions within NYAN

Membership Requirements

  • Nigerian citizen aged 18-40 years
  • Commitment to NYAN's vision and mission
  • Willingness to actively participate in NYAN activities
  • Passport photograph (recent, taken within 6 months)
  • Valid means of identification
  • Payment of membership registration fee
  • Adherence to NYAN's code of conduct

About Us

The Nigerian Youth Assembly for National Development (NYAN) is a non-partisan organization dedicated to empowering Nigerian youth through leadership development, community engagement, and civic education.
