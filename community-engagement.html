Our Engagement Initiatives

The Nigerian Youth Assembly for National Development (NYAN) is committed to fostering positive change through community engagement. Our initiatives focus on education, health, environmental sustainability, and economic empowerment to create lasting impact across Nigeria.

About Us

The Nigerian Youth Assembly for National Development (NYAN) is a non-partisan youth organization committed to fostering leadership, civic engagement, and national development among Nigerian youth.
