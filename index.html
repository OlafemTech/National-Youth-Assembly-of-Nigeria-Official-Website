Welcome to the National Youth Assembly of Nigeria (NYAN)

Who We Are

The National Youth Assembly of Nigeria (NYAN) is an independent youth Centric organisation with over a decade of service with the aim of serving as a Legislative body for the voice of the youth in Nigeria and also serve as a simulator to enlighten youth and women about governance, entrepreneurship and innovation to contribute towards unity and National development.

The association was founded by a group of patriotic, vibrant, charismatic and professional Nigerian youths who believe in the Nigerian state and are committed to making Nigeria the country of our dream through active youth engagement and positive participation working in collaboration with the Ministry of youth, and other relevant stakeholders in the Nigeria. particularly those engaged in the implementation of the Nigerian National youth policy.

Building a Stronger Youth Voice

Dear Fellow Youth Leaders and Change-makers,

Welcome to the National Youth Assembly of Nigeria (NYAN), where we believe in the power of young voices to shape our nation's future. Our assembly stands as a beacon of hope, unity, and progressive change in Nigeria.

Together, we are building a platform that amplifies youth voices, nurtures leadership, and drives positive transformation across our great nation. Through our collective efforts, we continue to demonstrate that young Nigerians are not just leaders of tomorrow, but active architects of today's progress.

Guiding Principles for Impact

Our Core Values

Youth Empowerment

Equipping young Nigerians with leadership, entrepreneurial, and governance skills to drive national development.

Integrity & Accountability

Promoting transparency, ethical leadership, and responsibility in all youth engagements and governance.

Unity & National Development

Fostering collaboration among young Nigerians to build a united and progressive nation.

Innovation & Creativity

Encouraging problem-solving and creative thinking to drive sustainable youth-led initiatives.

Youth Advocacy

Championing the rights, aspirations, and welfare of young Nigerians by influencing policies and decisions that impact the youth.

Good Governance

Promoting youth participation in leadership while fostering transparency, accountability, and ethical governance in Nigeria.

National Youth Assembly of Nigeria

The National Youth Assembly of Nigeria (NYAN) is an independent, youth-centric organization with over a decade of service, acting as a legislative voice for Nigerian youth.
