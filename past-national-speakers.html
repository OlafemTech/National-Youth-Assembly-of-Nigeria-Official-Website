Honoring Our Legacy

Past National Speakers

The Nigerian Youth Assembly for National Development (NYAN) has been led by visionary speakers who have contributed significantly to youth empowerment and national development in Nigeria. We honor their legacy and continued impact on our organization.

1st
2012-2014

Rt. Hon. Kassim M Kassim

1st NYAN Speaker

Led the inaugural assembly and established the foundation for NYAN's operations and advocacy work across Nigeria.

2nd
2014-2016

Rt. Hon. Luqman Majidadi

2nd NYAN Speaker

Expanded NYAN's reach to all states in Nigeria and initiated the first Youth Leadership Summit for national development.

3rd
2016-2021

Rt. Hon. Akhigbe Olumhense

3rd NYAN Speaker

Served two terms, extended due to COVID-19 pandemic. Established international partnerships and represented Nigerian youth at global forums.

4th
2021-2023

Rt. Hon. Mark Onuoha

4th NYAN Speaker

Focused on youth entrepreneurship and skills development programs across Nigeria. Led post-pandemic recovery initiatives for Nigerian youth.

5th
2023-Present

Rt. Hon. Usman Bashir Shagari

5th NYAN Speaker Current

Leading NYAN's digital transformation and expanding online engagement with Nigerian youth. Driving innovation in youth advocacy and development.

National Youth Assembly of Nigeria

The Nigerian Youth Assembly for National Development (NYAN) is a non-partisan, youth-focused organization dedicated to empowering young Nigerians and promoting their active participation in national development.
