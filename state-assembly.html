National Youth Assembly of Nigeria

The National Youth Assembly of Nigeria (NYAN) is an independent, youth-centric organization with over a decade of service, acting as a legislative voice for Nigerian youth. It fosters awareness on governance, entrepreneurship, and innovation, empowering youth and women to drive unity and national development.
