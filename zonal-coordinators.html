The Nigeria's Six (6) Geopolitical Zones

Nigeria is divided into six geopolitical zones, each grouping states with similar political, cultural, and economic ties. These zones help in administrative planning, resource allocation, and representation. Explore the map to see the states within each zone.

Jigawa | Kaduna | Kano | Katsina | Kebbi | Sokoto | Zamfara

North Central

Benue | Kogi | Kwara | Nasarawa | Niger | Plateau | Federal Capital Territory (FCT - Abuja)

South West

North East

South East

South South

National Youth Assembly of Nigeria

The National Youth Assembly of Nigeria (NYAN) is an independent, youth-centric organization with over a decade of service, acting as a legislative voice for Nigerian youth. It fosters awareness on governance, entrepreneurship, and innovation, empowering youth and women to drive unity and national development.
